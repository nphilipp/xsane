Preview window

The preview window is to select an area that you want to scan. You can resize the preview window and zoom into the scan area.
 
 
 
 


  Pipette white:
Use mouse pointer and click a point that shall be white with the left mouse button. You can abort the selection by pressing another mouse button.
  Pipette gray:
Use mouse pointer and click a point that shall be medium gray with the left mouse button. You can abort the selection by pressing another mouse button.
  Pipette black:
Use mouse pointer and click a point that shall be black with the left mouse button. You can abort the selection by pressing another mouse button.
   Unzoom:
Use full scan area.
   Zoom out:
Increase preview area by 20%.
   Zoom in:
Zoom into selected area.
   Undo zoom:
Undo last zoom.
Acquire Preview:
Start preview scan. If you resized the preview window, the new preview is done with updated resolution.
Cancel Preview:
Cancel a preview scan.
Left mouse button:
Select scan area: Press (&hold) button do define one edge, move the mouse so that the area you whis to scan is in the frame and release the button.
Edit scan area: Move the pointer over an edge of the existing selection and press (&hold)  the left mouse button, move the mouse and release the button.
Right and middle mouse button:
If you press (&hold) the right or the middle mouse button in the selection frame you can move the selection frame without resizing it.
